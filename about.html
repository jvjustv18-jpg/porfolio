Biography

I am Justin Villanueva, a dedicated student and youth leader from Barangay Dau, Mabalacat City, Pampanga. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Office Administration at Clark College of Science and Technology.

As the PYAP President, I actively lead programs and activities that aim to inspire, guide, and empower the youth in our community.

I strongly believe that leadership is about service, responsibility, and creating positive change. Through my experiences in school and community activities, I continue to grow as a student, leader, and future professional.
